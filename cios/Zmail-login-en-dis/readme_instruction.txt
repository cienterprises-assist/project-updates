To manage Zmail login/logout:
1. Log in as Administrator.
2. Copy zmail-res-log-in-out.exe and zmail-res-log-restrict.exe to C:\Windows\System32 or C:\Windows\System32\zmail-permit.
3. If antivirus flags, whitelist in K7, Quick Heal, or Windows Defender (Settings > Exclusions > Add File).
4. Open Command Prompt as Administrator.
5. To permit: zmail-res-log-in-out
6. To restrict: zmail-res-log-restrict
7. A pop-up confirms success or shows an error code.
8. Contact ci enterprises technical team for errors.
